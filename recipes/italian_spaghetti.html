Italian Spaghetti recipe

Homepage

Description

This is an Italian-style pasta sauce with homemade meatballs. You can make the sauce beforehand to save some time

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 cup fresh bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 table spoon grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 egg, beaten

Sauce

  • /4 cup chopped onion
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 (28 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 3/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, parley, Parmesan, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, garlic powder and beaten egg. Mix well and form into 12 balls, Store, covered, in refrigerator until needed.
  3. In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onions are translucent
  4. Stir in tomatoes, salt, sugar and bay leaf. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 90 minutes
  5. Stir in tomato paste, basil, 1/2 teaspoon pepper and meatballs and simmer 30 minuets more.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
